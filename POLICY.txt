Privacy Policy for G2B Table Extractor

Last Updated: August 29, 2025

Thank you for using G2B Table Extractor. This privacy policy explains how our extension handles your data.

1. Data We Access
When you activate the extension on a webpage, it requires access to read the content of that page. This is necessary to find and extract the data from the HTML table you wish to export.

2. How We Use Your Data
The data accessed from the webpage is used for the single purpose of generating an Excel (.xlsx) file from a table. This file is then downloaded directly to your computer for your own use.

3. Data Storage and Transmission
We take your privacy very seriously. The G2B Table Extractor extension:
- Does NOT collect, save, or store any of your personal information or browsing data.
- Does NOT transmit any data from your browser to our servers or any third-party servers.
- All data processing (reading the table and creating the Excel file) occurs entirely locally on your computer.

4. Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy in the future. We will notify you of any changes by updating the policy within the extension's store listing.
